Citing AIMM
===========

If you reference AIMM in your research please consider citing the paper that
specifies its architecture and interfaces. The paper is available `here
<https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9734070>`_. You can also use the
following BiBTeX entry:

.. code-block:: bibtex
    
    @ARTICLE{9734070,
      author={Sičanica, Zlatan and Sučić, Stjepan and Milašinović, Boris},
      journal={IEEE Access},
      title={Architecture of an Artificial Intelligence Model Manager for Event-Driven Component-Based SCADA Systems},
      year={2022},
      volume={10},
      number={},
      pages={30414-30426},
      doi={10.1109/ACCESS.2022.3159715}}
